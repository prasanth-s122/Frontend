The Great World Tree


Yggdrasil, the great ash tree, binds the universe together. Within its sprawling branches rest the Nine Realms of Norse Mythology. Though they share the same physical universe space, they occupy different dimensions layered on top of one another. The only way to traverse between them is through the great travel rooms built by Tyr, requiring immense magical energy from the Bifröst.

Select a realm below to delve deep into its ancient history and secrets.
