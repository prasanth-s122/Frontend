Niflheim

The Realm of Mist and Fog

Niflheim, meaning the "Mist Home", is the primordial realm of ice, crushing cold, and freezing fog. Long ago, it was a relatively peaceful realm of pure frost until it was corrupted by the dark dwarf alchemist Ivaldi. He sought to harness the realm's cold to create terrible cursed mists that now poison anyone wandering through its labyrinths.


Ivaldi's Workshop

At the center of the deadly fog lies the ruined remnants of Ivaldi's Workshop. Disgraced and punished by the gods, Ivaldi sealed himself along with vast mythical treasures. The workshop is protected by a procedurally shifting maze of death traps and poisonous fumes. Exploring the maze requires specialized armor and quick thinking, as the mist actively seeks out heat and life to extinguish.


The Frozen Wastes

Beyond the cursed maze, Niflheim offers little hospitality. It houses ancient primordial glaciers and acts as a prison for some of the most dangerous entities the Aesir sought to bury. It is a bleak, unwelcoming environment that stands as a stark reminder of unchecked ambition and the consequences of meddling with primordial forces.
