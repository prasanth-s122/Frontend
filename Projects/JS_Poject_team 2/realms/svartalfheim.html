Svartalfheim

The Realm of the Dwarves

Svartalfheim is highly advanced, sprawling, and heavily industrialized. It is the historic homeland of the Dwarves, masters of smithing and magical machinery. The realm is characterized by huge mining operations, complex winding aqueducts, massive water wheels, and intricate dwarven machinery built seamlessly into the mountains and earth.


The Forges of Creation

Though largely oppressed by the Aesir and forced to mine resources to fund Odin's endless preparations for Ragnarok, the dwarves of Svartalfheim remain incredibly resilient. The realm constantly pumps smoke and steam into the green skies from their massive forges. These forges are responsible for crafting the most powerful weapons in existence, including Thor's hammer, Mjölnir, and Kratos' Leviathan Axe.


The Great Taverns and Mines

Before the Aesir occupation, Svartalfheim was a lively realm filled with boastful taverns, lively trade ports, and impressive architectural feats. Deep within its sprawling underground mines sleep creatures corrupted by industrial waste, such as the monstrous Dreki and Grim. Despite the smog and forced labor, the dwarven spirit shines bright in the hidden rebellions scattered across the wetlands.
