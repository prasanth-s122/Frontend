Muspelheim

The Realm of Fire

Muspelheim is a primordial, actively hostile realm entirely constructed of rivers of lava, jagged obsidian, and falling ash. It is one of the two original realms (the other being Niflheim) from which all creation sprang, born from the great void Ginnungagap. It burns with intense heat that drives away any who lack divine strength to brave it.


The Crucible of Surtr

At the center of this volcanic nightmare sits the massive Fire Giant, Surtr, the ruler of Muspelheim. He waits patiently under ground, forging a massive sword required for the day of Ragnarok, where he is destined to reduce Asgard to ashes. The realm serves as a massive trial by fire, often attracting warriors seeking to prove their valor through increasingly impossible magical combat trials orchestrated by the realm itself.


The Primordial Inferno

There is virtually no civilization in Muspelheim; only pure elemental destruction. Small pockets of life exist only in the form of monstrous fire trolls, magma travelers, and mythical beasts forged from the magma. Traversing Muspelheim requires constant vigilance, as the tectonic plates constantly shift and erupt violently.
