Midgard

The Realm of Mortals

Midgard is the mortal realm and essentially the focal point of the Nine Realms. Once a lush, diverse landscape, it has been plunged into the deep, bitter winter known as Fimbulwinter, signaling the onset of Ragnarok.


The Lake of Nine

In the center of Midgard lies the Lake of Nine, home to Tyr's Temple and the massive World Serpent, Jörmungandr. The serpent is so large that it wraps its body around the entirety of the realm, its very size altering the water levels whenever it shifts. Tyr's Temple serves as the gateway through the World Tree, a masterpiece of ancient architecture currently frozen in ice.
