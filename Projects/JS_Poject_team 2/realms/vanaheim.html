Vanaheim

The Realm of the Vanir

Vanaheim is a dense, magical, and perilous jungle realm. Characterized by glowing, toxic flora, ancient overgrown ruins, and hostile wildlife, it is the home of the Vanir—gods connected to nature, magic, and clairvoyance.


Wild Untamed Magic

Unlike Midgard’s harsh winter or Alfheim’s serene deserts, Vanaheim is overflowing with untamed life. Magic pulsates through the air itself, visibly manifesting in floating spores, massive carnivorous plants, and skies that alternate between eternal day and night at a whim. The crater of Vanaheim reveals deep cosmic scars left over from the historic Aesir-Vanir war.
