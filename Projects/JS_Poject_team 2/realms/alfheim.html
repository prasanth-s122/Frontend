Alfheim

The Realm of the Elves

Alfheim, the breathtaking realm of both Light and Dark Elves, is characterized by its ethereal beauty, glowing alien flora, and striking elven architecture. Located beyond the lower branches of Yggdrasil, it is an ever-shifting landscape defined by an ancient, endless war. Alfheim is a place of breathtaking serenity that acts only as a facade for the brutal, underlying conflict that ravages its inhabitants.


The Light of Alfheim

The very heart of the realm is the Light of Alfheim, a massive central pillar of pure magical energy that grants vitality, insight, and power to whoever controls it. Currently, it is locked in a fierce tug-of-war between the Light Elves and the Dark Elves. The Light is said to contain the souls of the dead waiting for rebirth. The continuous war for control affects not only the physical land but the atmosphere itself; whoever holds the Light literally shapes Alfheim’s weather and geography.


The Deserts and Temples

Beyond the Ringed Temple that houses the Light, the realm expands into sprawling, barren deserts formed by centuries of war over an ancient beast called the Hafgufa. These brutal sands hide massive cave systems containing secrets of the early elven race before they fractured into "Light" and "Dark". Exploring Alfheim requires cunning and magic, as the Light logic puzzles and the hostile native flora can easily overwhelm trespassers.
