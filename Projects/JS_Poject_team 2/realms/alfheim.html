Alfheim

The Realm of the Elves

Alfheim, the breathtaking realm of both Light and Dark Elves, is characterized by its ethereal beauty, glowing flora, and striking architecture. Located beyond the branches of Yggdrasil, it is an ever-shifting landscape defined by an endless, ancient war.


The Light of Alfheim

The very heart of the realm is the Light of Alfheim, a pillar of pure energy that grants vitality to whoever controls it. Currently, it is locked in a fierce, never-ending tug-of-war between the Light Elves and the Dark Elves. Every time the light changes hands, the landscape of the realm morphs drastically, transforming to reflect its new masters.
